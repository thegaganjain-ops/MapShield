Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services

Our pricing varies based on the complexity of your case. Simple reinstatements start at a lower price point, while more complex cases with multiple suspensions or policy violations may require our premium package. Contact us for a free assessment and exact quote.

Most reinstatements are completed within 3-7 business days after we submit all required documentation. However, complex cases may take up to 2 weeks. We'll provide you with a realistic timeline after reviewing your specific situation.

While no one can guarantee 100% reinstatement (as final approval comes from Google), we have a 95% success rate with properly documented cases. If we're unable to get your profile reinstated after following all procedures correctly, we offer partial refunds based on the work completed.

We'll need access to your Google Business Profile (if still accessible), business documentation (license, registration, utility bills, etc.), and details about your suspension notice. All information is kept strictly confidential and only used for reinstatement purposes.
